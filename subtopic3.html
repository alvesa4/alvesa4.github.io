The Impact of Technology in the Workplace
Site Author

Abstract
 This article focuses on the impacts of technology in the workplace. It explains how organizations need to adapt to new technologies, like Intelligent automation and artificial intelligence, to achieve more remarkable ability, make it possible to do more with fewer resources, increase profitability, and keep up with competitive business for the next generation. It tells how technology transformed the work environment and productivity by giving the disadvantages and advantages of integrating technology in the workplace.
