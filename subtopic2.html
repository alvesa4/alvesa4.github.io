URL
How Digital Technology Changed the Way We Consume & Process News
Site Author
 LinkedIn
Abstract
 This site discusses how digital technology has significantly shifted from traditional media to get news and information to smartphones, social media, and AI-powered platforms. It outlines how they provide instant information access that can lead to information overload, meaning it affects mental focus, confusion, and increases anxiety in a person. It talks about the troubles with the information bubble created by algorithms that limit people to all types of perspectives of the news and information, creating a risk of bias and misinformation. It explains the opportunities and challenges with the rise of AI and how to manage endless newsfeeds from impacting personal well-being by consuming information mindfully and taking regular breaks to reflect and process everything.
